You missed several very good free-key/free-credit routes. I’d rank the **biggest misses** as: **Vercel AI Gateway, Kilo Gateway, Z.ai/BigModel, SambaNova Cloud, direct Anthropic, direct OpenAI, Cohere, Hugging Face Inference Providers, Azure AI Foundry, Google Vertex/Agent Platform, Ollama Cloud**.

I’m excluding the providers you already listed as the **API-key issuer**. Some underlying model creators may overlap, but the key/free-hosting route below is not one of your current keys.

### Free pools worth adding

**Vercel AI Gateway** is the highest-leverage miss: `AI_GATEWAY_API_KEY`, $5/month free credits, all available models, no markup, and usable without the underlying provider account. Vercel says the monthly free credits start on the first AI Gateway request and stop if you buy paid credits. ([Vercel][1])

**Kilo Gateway** is the best truly-free coding gateway: `KILO_API_KEY`, plus anonymous access for `:free` models at **200 requests/hour/IP**. Kilo lists examples like `minimax/minimax-m2.1:free` and `z-ai/glm-5:free`, and its free plan is $0/forever. ([Kilo][2])

**SambaNova Cloud** gives `SAMBANOVA_API_KEY` with **$5 free API credits**, no credit card, production models, and the initial credits expire in 30 days. Its listed models include DeepSeek V3.2, GPT-OSS-120B, Llama 4 Maverick, MiniMax M2.5/M2.7, and DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Llama-70B. ([SambaNova Cloud][3])

**Z.ai / BigModel** is a strong direct miss for GLM models: the site advertises **20 million free tokens** and highlights coding capability. ([zhipuai.cn][4])

**Cohere** gives `COHERE_API_KEY` trial keys where trial API calls are free, limited to **1,000 API calls/month** and, for Command A / Command A Reasoning, **20 req/min**. ([Cohere Documentation][5])

**Hugging Face Inference Providers** gives `HF_TOKEN` and monthly routed-provider credits: **$0.10/month for free users**, tiny but recurring, across 200+ models. ([Hugging Face][6])

**Anthropic direct** gives `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY`; Anthropic says new users receive “a small amount of free credits,” but it does not publish the amount on the pricing page. ([Claude][7])

**OpenAI direct** gives `OPENAI_API_KEY`; OpenAI still documents an “Explore free trial” plan with limited functionality, but the exact credit/access varies by account. ([OpenAI Help Center][8])

**Azure AI Foundry** is a free-credit route, not a permanent free tier: $200 credit for 30 days, with hosted OpenAI, DeepSeek, xAI Grok, Llama, Cohere, Kimi, Mistral, Fireworks, and more; Foundry also has quota tiers including a Free Tier. ([Microsoft Azure][9])

**Google Vertex / Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform** is another credit route: up to $300 free credits for new customers, and Claude models are fully managed/serverless through Vertex AI APIs. This is separate from a simple Gemini AI Studio key. ([Google Cloud][10])
